This shoe reconvenes the creative partners on yet another AM1 collaboration. The shoe’s defining feature is a wavy suede mudguard, rendered in a bright blue finish with an embroidered mini-Swoosh on the lateral forefoot. The rest of the upper is crafted from matte silver mesh with white leather overlays and a blue suede Swoosh. A woven Patta tag graces the mesh tongue, while embroidered Nike Air branding adorns the back heel. Lightweight cushioning is provided by a pre-yellowed polyurethane midsole with a visible Max Air unit nestled
under the heel.
